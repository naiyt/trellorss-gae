A note on privacy: The token will give permission for this app to read your Trello boards for 30 days (or until you revoke its access). All it does is query the API for new actions on your boards every 30 minutes, and create an RSS feed for it. No data from your boards is stored permanently -- it's all stored in memcache, and it's cleared every 30 minutes so that new updates can be cached.

This app won't do anything evil with your data, but please not that once you've put up a feed, that feed is publicly accessible to anyone with the url! Thus, if you have any boards with potentially confidential info, I recommend agains creating an RSS feed for it here.

Or if you prefer, you can even grab the source for this app, and host it on your own Google App Engine instance.
